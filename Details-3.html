How to make KFC egg tart

Egg tart with crispy crust and greasy inside is a favorite of many people. This egg tart requires no expensive ingredients, just a little patience. Let's go to the kitchen to make delicious egg tarts that are not inferior to KFC right here!

Ingredients for making KFC egg tart

  • Multiware Myte: 400 gr
  • Slightly salty margarine: 200 gr
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon
  • Yolk: 2 Yolks
  • Egg: 3 eggs
  • Condensed milk: 60 ml
  • Non-sugar fresh milk: 200 ml
  • Vani: 1 teaspoon

Implementation tools

Microwave, oven, bowl, gloves, rolling pin.

How to processing KFC egg tart

  1. Kneading and rolling the dough

    First, you measure 100g of all-purpose flour into a bowl, add 200g of Margarine butter and knead well.
    Spoon the butter mixture into plastic wrap, wrap into cubes, and place in the freezer for 15 minutes.
    In the meantime, add 300g of all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon of sugar, 2 egg yolks, and 150ml of water, respectively, into a bowl, knead until the mixture is smooth.
    We proceed to roll the dough into a cross-shaped piece, not too thin.

  2. Package of pie crust

    Next, take the butter that has been cooled in the freezer compartment of the refrigerator, put it in the center of the dough, wrap and roll it gently, fold the dough into 3, then wrap it with plastic wrap, put it in the freezer for a while. 15 minutes.

  3. Make milk cream filling

    To make the egg tart filling, first mix 60ml of condensed milk with 200ml of unsweetened fresh milk in a bowl. Then spin the bowl in the microwave on Medium-High for 3 minutes.
    In another bowl, beat in 3 eggs and 1 teaspoon vanilla, stir to dissolve. Take the bowl of hot milk out of the microwave, then pour in the eggs, stir well, and then strain through a sieve to prevent the filling from getting lumpy.
    Finally, you let the milk cream rest for 15 minutes to let the bubbles disappear, this helps the egg tart filling to be more smooth.

  4. Finish the pie crust

    Back to the crust, take the dough out of the fridge, roll it out, fold it into 3, then wrap the wrapper and put it back in the freezer for 15 minutes.
    After 15 minutes, you repeat the above steps, then do the rolling, folding and freezing again, in total we have rolled and folded the dough 4 times. The total number of layers rolled is 3x3x3x3=81 layers.

  5. Bake

    In the 5th roll of the dough, roll the dough to only 3mm thin, cut the dough into round pieces with a mold and then line it in the baking pan. The egg tart shell is ready.
    Before baking, preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius. Carefully pour the milky filling into the crust and place the baking tray in the center slot of the oven for the most even heat distribution.
    Bake the egg tart for 20 to 25 minutes, watch until the crust is golden brown, then remove. This is a very simple and easy way to make KFC egg tarts, and it's also delicious.

  6. Finished product

    Fragrant, soft egg tarts with a crispy crust and a creamy custard filling will knock anyone out.
